About CrowdSense

CrowdSense is a smart tourism intelligence platform designed to help visitors plan their trips to iconic landmarks with data-driven confidence. Using the Holt-Winters Triple Exponential Smoothing algorithm — a time series forecasting method that accounts for trends, seasonality, and level changes — we analyze 3+ years of crowd data to predict congestion levels at any given time.

We use triple exponential smoothing to capture daily, weekly, and monthly crowd patterns.
